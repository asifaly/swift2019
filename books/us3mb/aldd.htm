C1 

If the message is a cancellation, that is, field 23G is CANC, then subsequence A1 must be present at least once, and a reference to the previous message must be specified in the Linkage section, that is, field :20C::PREV must be present at least once in that message (Error code(s): E08).

C2 

In sequence C, fields 16R and 16S may not be the only fields present. If both fields 16R and 16S are present, then at least one other field of the same sequence must be present (Error code(s): D13).
