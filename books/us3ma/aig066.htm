MT 306 Field Specifications

66. Field 37U: Trigger Level

FORMAT

Option U 12d (Rate)

PRESENCE

Mandatory in conditional (see rule C9) sequence G

DEFINITION

This field specifies the trigger level. For double triggers, this field specifies the upper level.

NETWORK VALIDATED RULES

The integer part of Rate must contain at least one digit. A decimal comma is mandatory and is included in the maximum length (Error code(s): T40,T43).

USAGE RULES

If sequence G is present more than once in the message then, in order to facilitate consistency between the two parties, it is recommended that the repetitions are ordered such that the values in field 37U are presented in increasing order.
