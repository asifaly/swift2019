MT 565 Field Specifications

48. Field 70E: Narrative

FORMAT

Option E :4!c//10*35x (Qualifier)(Narrative)

PRESENCE

Optional in mandatory sequence D

QUALIFIER

(Error code(s): T89)

Order M/O Qualifier R/N CR Options Qualifier Description
1 O COMP R   E Information to be Complied With
2 O DLVR R   E Delivery Details
3 O FXIN R   E FX Instructions Narrative
4 O INST R C2 E Corporate Action Instructions Narrative

DEFINITION

This qualified generic field specifies:

COMP Information to be Complied With Provides information conditions to the account owner that are to be complied with, for example, not open to US/Canadian residents, QIB or SIL to be provided.
DLVR Delivery Details Provides additional information on the delivery details of the out-turned (derived) securities. This narrative is only to be used in case the securities are not eligible at the agent/custodian, and may not be used for settlement instructions.
FXIN FX Instructions Narrative Provides additional details pertaining to foreign exchange instructions.
INST Corporate Action Instructions Narrative Provides additional details pertaining to the corporate action instruction.

USAGE RULES

Unless bilaterally agreed, narrative field 70a must not contain information that can be provided in a structured field.
