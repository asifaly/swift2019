C1 

If the message is a cancellation, that is, Function of the Message (field 23G) is CANC, then subsequence A1 (Linkages) must be present at least once in the message, and in one and only one occurrence of A1, field :20C::PREV must be present; consequently, in all other occurrences of A1, field :20C::PREV is not allowed (Error code(s): E08).

NEWM Optional Not applicable

C6 

In each occurrence of sequence C:

  • Field :95L::OWND must not be present more than once.

  • Field :95P::OWND must not be present more than once.

  • Field :95R::OWND must not be present more than once.

  • Field :95V::OWND must not be present more than once.

And one of the following fields :95P::OWND or :95R::OWND or :95V::OWND must be present.

Field :95L::OWND may be present only if one of the fields :95P::OWND or :95R::OWND or :95V::OWND is present (Error code(s): E77) .
